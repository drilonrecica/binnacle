# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the TALOS community respectful and free of
harassment, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity or expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other personal
characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while discussing technical disagreement.
- Give and receive feedback about code and ideas, not people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized content or advances,
doxxing, sustained disruption, or other conduct that a reasonable person would
consider unprofessional or unsafe is not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report incidents privately to the project maintainer using the contact route in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be handled promptly and as
confidentially as practical. The maintainer may warn, remove content,
temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban a participant depending
on severity and pattern.

Retaliation against a reporter or a person participating in an investigation is
not tolerated.
